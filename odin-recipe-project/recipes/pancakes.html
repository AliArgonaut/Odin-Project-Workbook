Pancakes

Description

Step into a world of breakfast bliss with our heavenly pancake recipe that promises to elevate your mornings to a whole new level of delight. These pancakes are not just a breakfast item; they're a celebration on a plate, embodying the perfect balance of fluffy texture, golden perfection, and sweet indulgence. Get ready to embark on a culinary journey that transforms the ordinary into extraordinary with every delectable bite.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Unsalted butter
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar, creating the foundation of your pancake batter.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and then add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Stir until the wet ingredients are well combined.
  3. Gently fold the wet ingredients into the dry mixture, ensuring a smooth batter. Be careful not to overmix, as a few lumps contribute to the pancake's fluffiness.
  4. Preheat a griddle or non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or cooking spray.
  5. Using a ladle or measuring cup, pour the batter onto the hot griddle, forming round pancakes. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Stack the pancakes on a plate, creating a tower of pillowy goodness.
