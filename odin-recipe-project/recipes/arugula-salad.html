Arugula Salad

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh arugula, washed and dried
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 1 avocado, sliced

For the Lemon Vinaigrette

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup (optional for sweetness)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large salad bowl, place the fresh arugula, ensuring it is washed and thoroughly dried.
  2. Scatter the halved cherry tomatoes, thinly sliced red onion, crumbled feta cheese, and toasted pine nuts over the arugula.
  3. Gently slice the avocado and arrange the slices on top of the salad.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil, fresh lemon juice, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, and honey or maple syrup (if using). Season the vinaigrette with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the balance of flavors as needed.
  5. Drizzle the lemon vinaigrette over the salad, ensuring an even coating. Toss the salad gently to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Transfer the salad to a serving platter or individual plates. Optionally, garnish with an extra sprinkle of feta cheese or pine nuts. Serve immediately and enjoy the zesty and refreshing flavors of this arugula salad.
